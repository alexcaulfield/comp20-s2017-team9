About

PopQuiz is an interactive DJ game created by masterminds DJ Trac, DJ Cones, DJ Two Sheep, DJ Dillweed, and DJ Drano. This game uses the Spotify API to both play songs and find the values for certain characteristics of the song such as danceability, beats per minute, energy, and many more. These song characteristics are then used to determine the likeness between two songs and give you, the DJ, a rating for how well you lined up your personal DJ set.
